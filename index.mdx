Ice Cubes is a free, open-source Mastodon client crafted entirely in SwiftUI. It runs natively on iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and visionOS, offering a rich social experience with deep customization, real-time streaming, multi-account support, and home screen widgets.

## What makes Ice Cubes special

Ice Cubes is designed to feel like a first-party Apple app — fast, native, and accessible across every Apple platform. It goes beyond the basics with features like tag groups (custom multi-hashtag timelines), remote local timelines, AI-assisted post composition, and a privacy-preserving push notification proxy.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Ice Cubes">
    Get Ice Cubes free from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ice-cubes-for-mastodon/id6444915884) on your iPhone, iPad, Mac, or Apple Vision Pro.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to your Mastodon account">
    Open Ice Cubes, browse and select your Mastodon instance, and sign in securely using Apple's WebAuthenticationSession. Your access token is stored in the system keychain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your timeline">
    Your home timeline loads immediately with cached posts. Switch between home, local, federated, and trending timelines from the navigation bar title menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your experience">
    Head to Settings to choose a theme, adjust fonts and spacing, configure swipe gestures, and set up push notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>
