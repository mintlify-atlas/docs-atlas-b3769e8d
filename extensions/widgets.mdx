title: "Home Screen Widgets for Ice Cubes"
description: "Add Ice Cubes widgets to your iPhone, iPad, or Mac home screen: latest posts, mentions, hashtag feeds, list timelines, and account info."
---

Ice Cubes includes a suite of home screen widgets that keep your Mastodon content visible at a glance without opening the app. Each widget is powered by App Intents, so you can configure your account, timeline, list, or hashtag directly from the widget editing interface.

## Available widgets

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Latest posts">
    Displays the most recent posts from a timeline you choose. Supports your home timeline, local timeline, federated timeline, or any other timeline filter available in Ice Cubes.

    **Supported sizes:** Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large

    **Configuration:** Account, Timeline

    - Small and Medium show the single most recent post.
    - Large and Extra Large show up to five recent posts.
    - Boosts and posts with no text content are filtered out automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mentions">
    Shows your most recent @-mentions so you can see who has replied to or tagged you without launching the app.

    **Supported sizes:** Large, Extra Large

    **Configuration:** Account

    The widget fetches up to five recent mention notifications and displays the post content alongside the author's avatar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Hashtag timeline">
    Streams the latest public posts for any hashtag you specify.

    **Supported sizes:** Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large

    **Configuration:** Account, Hashtag

    Enter any hashtag (without the `#` prefix) when configuring the widget. The widget title displays the tag prefixed with `#`.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="List timeline">
    Shows the most recent posts from one of your Mastodon lists.

    **Supported sizes:** Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large

    **Configuration:** Account, List

    Your available lists are fetched from the selected account and presented as a picker during widget configuration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account">
    Displays your profile avatar and your current follower count at a glance.

    **Supported sizes:** Small only

    **Configuration:** Account

    The widget verifies credentials on each refresh so the follower count stays current.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to add a widget

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter edit mode">
    On iPhone or iPad, long-press an empty area of your home screen until the icons start to jiggle. On Mac (Sonoma or later), Control-click the desktop and choose **Edit Widgets**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the widget gallery">
    Tap or click the **+** button in the top-left corner to open the widget gallery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Ice Cubes">
    Scroll through the list or type **Ice Cubes** in the search field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a widget and size">
    Select the widget type you want (Latest posts, Mentions, etc.) and swipe through the available size options, then tap **Add Widget**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the widget">
    Tap the newly placed widget while still in edit mode (or long-press it later and choose **Edit Widget**) to set the account, timeline, hashtag, or list for that widget.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Widget configuration

Each widget uses App Intents to present a native configuration UI. The parameters available depend on the widget type:

| Widget | Account | Timeline | Hashtag | List |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Latest posts | Required | Required | — | — |
| Mentions | Required | — | — | — |
| Hashtag timeline | Required | — | Required | — |
| List timeline | Required | — | — | Required |
| Account | Required | — | — | — |

Tap a widget and choose **Edit Widget** to change any of these settings at any time. If you have multiple Mastodon accounts in Ice Cubes, the Account picker lists all of them.

## Supported widget sizes

Widget size support varies by widget type. The table below maps each widget to the WidgetKit family it supports, as declared in the source:

| Widget | Small | Medium | Large | Extra Large |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Latest posts | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Mentions | — | — | Yes | Yes |
| Hashtag timeline | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| List timeline | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Account | Yes | — | — | — |

<Note>
  Extra Large widgets are available on iPad home screens and on Mac (macOS Sonoma and later).
</Note>

## App Intents integration

Ice Cubes ships an `IceCubesAppIntents` target that exposes Siri and Shortcuts actions alongside the widget configuration intents. The available App Shortcuts let you:

- **Compose a post** — open the Ice Cubes composer, optionally pre-filled with text passed from a previous Shortcut step.
- **Send a post (inline)** — send a post entirely within a Shortcut without opening the app.
- **Send image(s) (background)** — attach one or more photos and post them without switching to the app.
- **Post a status with an image** — open the composer pre-loaded with a photo.
- **Open Ice Cubes on a tab** — navigate directly to a specific tab such as Home Timeline, Notifications, Mentions, Explore, Bookmarks, Favorites, Lists, or Trending Links.

All intents are available in the Shortcuts app and can be invoked via Siri using natural phrases such as "Post in Ice Cubes" or "Open Ice Cubes."
